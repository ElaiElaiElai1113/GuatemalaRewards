# Canva Guide - Business Transactions and Gift Cards

Purpose: use this content to create a Canva training deck for business staff, business owners, and the Spanish operations team.

Recommended Canva format:

- Presentation, 16:9.
- 12 slides.
- Simple text.
- One process per slide.
- Use screenshots from the business Transactions page.
- Add arrows or circles around the buttons staff must click.

## Slide 1 - Business Transactions Training

Title:
Business Transactions Training

Subtitle:
How to process normal sales and gift-card sales in Guatemala Rewards.

Main points:

- Staff use the Transactions page.
- Staff can process a sale with or without a gift card.
- The system calculates customer total, points, and commission.

Visual:
Screenshot of `/business/redemptions`.

Speaker note:
This guide is for business staff. It explains the exact steps to process a customer purchase.

## Slide 2 - What Staff Need Before Starting

Title:
Before You Start

Main points:

- Customer Member QR.
- Receipt or bill number.
- Bill before tax and service charge.
- Optional gift card QR, link, or code.

Visual:
Four simple icons or screenshot callouts:

- QR.
- Receipt.
- Bill amount.
- Gift card.

Speaker note:
The most important thing is the customer Member QR. The gift card is optional.

## Slide 3 - Open Transactions

Title:
Open the Transactions Page

Steps:

1. Sign in to the Business Portal.
2. Open Transactions.
3. Use this page for normal transactions and gift-card transactions.

Route:
`/business/redemptions`

Visual:
Screenshot of business navigation with Transactions highlighted.

Speaker note:
Even if the page route says redemptions, staff should think of this as the Transactions page.

## Slide 4 - Normal Transaction: Load the Customer

Title:
Step 1: Load the Customer

Steps:

1. Scan the customer Member QR.
2. Or paste the Member QR link/token.
3. Click Load Member.
4. Confirm the customer name appears.

Visual:
Screenshot of the Member QR field and Load Member button.

Callout:
Confirm the correct customer before processing the sale.

## Slide 5 - Normal Transaction: Enter Sale Details

Title:
Step 2: Enter Sale Details

Steps:

1. Enter the bill before tax and service charge.
2. Enter the receipt or bill number.

Important:
The bill before tax/service is the base purchase amount.

Example:
If the food bill is 230, enter 230.

Visual:
Screenshot of bill amount and receipt fields.

## Slide 6 - Normal Transaction: Review the Calculation

Title:
Step 3: Review the Calculation

Check:

- Reward rate.
- Reward value.
- Points awarded.
- Tax added, if enabled.
- Service charge, if enabled.
- Customer total.
- Commission.

Rule:
Points are based on the bill before tax and service charge.

Visual:
Screenshot of Reward Calculation preview.

## Slide 7 - Normal Transaction: Process Without Gift Card

Title:
Step 4: Process Without Gift Card

Steps:

1. Check the customer and receipt.
2. Check the reward preview.
3. Click Process Without Gift Card.
4. Wait for success.
5. Click New Transaction for the next customer.

Visual:
Screenshot with Process Without Gift Card and New Transaction circled.

Speaker note:
Use this flow when the customer is only making a normal purchase.

## Slide 8 - Gift Card Transaction: Add Gift Card

Title:
Gift Card Sale: Add the Gift Card

Steps:

1. Load the customer.
2. Enter bill amount.
3. Enter receipt number.
4. Scan, upload, paste, or type the gift card QR/link/code.

Visual:
Screenshot of optional gift card section.

Callout:
Gift card is optional. Only use it when the customer is paying with a gift card.

## Slide 9 - Gift Card Transaction: Validate Gift Card

Title:
Validate the Gift Card

Steps:

1. Click Validate Gift Card.
2. Confirm status says Active.
3. Confirm it belongs to this business.

If not active:

- Do not process the gift card.
- Ask the customer for another valid gift card.

Visual:
Screenshot with Validate Gift Card button and Active badge circled.

## Slide 10 - Gift Card Transaction: Understand the Math

Title:
How Gift Card Math Works

Simple rule:

- Gift card reduces the customer total.
- Points are still based on the bill before tax and service charge.
- Tax and service charge do not create reward points.

Example:

- Bill before tax/service: 230.
- Tax: 12.6%.
- Gift card: 230.
- Customer total after gift card: 28.98.
- Reward rate: 20%.
- Points awarded: 46.

Visual:
Use a simple math graphic or the Reward Calculation preview.

## Slide 11 - Gift Card Transaction: Process With Gift Card

Title:
Process With Gift Card

Steps:

1. Confirm gift card is Active.
2. Check gift card discount.
3. Check customer total.
4. Check points awarded.
5. Click Process With Gift Card.
6. Click New Transaction for the next customer.

Visual:
Screenshot with Process With Gift Card button circled.

Speaker note:
After processing, the gift card cannot be used again.

## Slide 12 - Review Transaction History

Title:
Review Transaction History

## Canva Design Direction

Style:

- Clean operational training deck.
- Warm Guatemala Rewards colors.
- Large screenshots.
- Short instructions.
- Use arrows, circles, and numbered steps.

Avoid:

- Long paragraphs.
- Technical database language.
- Too many steps on one slide.

Best use:

- Staff onboarding.
- Business owner demo.
- Spanish team training.
- Boss/client presentation.
